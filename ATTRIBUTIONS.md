# Video Tutorials Attribution & Legal Compliance

This document provides complete attribution for all video tutorials embedded in the Sudoku App. We are committed to respecting creators' rights and following YouTube's Terms of Service.

## Legal Framework

### YouTube Embedding Policy
- ✅ All videos are embedded using YouTube's official `youtube.com/embed/` and `youtube-nocookie.com/embed/` API
- ✅ Embedding respects copyright and YouTube's Terms of Service
- ✅ Revenue sharing remains with original creators
- ✅ No videos are downloaded or re-hosted locally

### Privacy Protection
- ✅ Using `youtube-nocookie.com` domain (EU privacy regulation compliant)
- ✅ No user tracking through YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode
- ✅ Proper `allow` and `sandbox` attributes on all iframes

### Attribution Standards
- ✅ All creators are clearly credited with clickable channel links
- ✅ Video titles and descriptions are preserved
- ✅ License types are explicitly displayed
- ✅ Educational/fair-use context is maintained

## Legal Compliance Checklist

✅ **Embedding Method**
- Uses official YouTube embed API
- Privacy-enhanced youtube-nocookie.com domain
- Proper iframe permissions (allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture")

✅ **Attribution**
- Creator names displayed prominently
- Clickable links to creator channels
- Video titles and descriptions shown
- License type clearly indicated

✅ **No Copyright Violations**
- No videos downloaded or re-hosted
- No removal of creator branding
- No modification of original content
- Revenue sharing remains with creators

✅ **Educational Purpose**
- Content used for learning, not entertainment
- Fair-use context maintained
- Non-commercial educational application
- Clear educational disclaimers

✅ **User Privacy**
- No tracking cookies from embedded videos
- Privacy-first embedding approach
- GDPR/EU compliance considerations
